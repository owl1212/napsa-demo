About TBZ

Regulating, promoting, and developing the tobacco industry since 1968.

Our History

Over 50 Years of Excellence

The Tobacco Board of Zambia (TBZ) was established under the Tobacco Act of 1967 (Chapter 237 of the Laws of Zambia). Our mandate is to regulate, promote, and develop the tobacco industry in Zambia.

  • Promoting orderly marketing in Lusaka, Eastern, and Southern Provinces
  • Enforcing quality standards (Zambia Good Agricultural Practices)
  • Maximizing value for Zambian farmers

Our Services

Comprehensive support for the entire value chain.
