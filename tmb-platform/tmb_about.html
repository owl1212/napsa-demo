Custodians of the Industry.

The Tobacco Board of Zambia (TBZ) is a statutory body established under the Tobacco Act, Cap 237 of the Laws of Zambia.

Our Core Mandate

We exist to regulate, promote, and develop the tobacco industry in Zambia.

1

Regulation

Licensing of growers, buyers, and floor operators to ensure compliance with Cap 237.

2

Arbitration

Resolving disputes on classification, pricing, and trade practices between growers and buyers.

3

Promotion

Marketing Zambian tobacco globally and ensuring sustainable production practices.
